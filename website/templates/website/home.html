Executive summary

SafeGuard AI is an AI-powered security suite designed to bridge the digital safety gap for seniors and persons with disabilities. By combining real-time scam detection with advanced accessibility features, the app empowers vulnerable populations to navigate the digital world without fear of financial exploitation.

The problem

  • The victimization gap: Seniors lost over $3.4 billion to fraud in 2023.
  • Accessibility barriers: Current security apps are too complex for seniors or lack support for blind/deaf users.
  • Language vulnerability: Non-English speaking seniors are often targeted due to a lack of localized protective tools.

The solution: key innovation

  • 🧠
    AI-powered analysis: Uses natural language processing to detect urgency, fear tactics, and impersonation.
  • 🎤
    Voice-first interface: Users can speak messages into the app instead of typing, facilitating ease of use for those with motor or visual impairments.
  • 🌐
    Universal safety: Multi-language detection and translation ensure protection regardless of the user's native tongue.

Technical features

Feature Benefit
Scam risk meter Visual 0–100 score for instant understanding.
Family link Notifies a trusted contact when a high-risk threat is detected.
Haptic feedback Unique vibration patterns for blind/deaf-blind accessibility.
Official verification One-touch connection to verified bank/government phone numbers.
Training feature Practice quizzes by scam type to build confidence.

Social impact & market potential

  • Financial safety: Direct reduction in scam victimization rates.
  • Digital confidence: Reducing the "fear factor" of technology for elderly users.
  • Scalability: Potential partnerships with senior living communities, banks, and disability advocacy groups.
