I Need Help

If you think you've been scammed, use these official U.S. government and phone resources only. Do not trust links from strangers or suspicious messages.

🔑

I gave my password

Change passwords immediately. identitytheft.gov is the official U.S. government site for identity theft and compromised accounts.

Go to IdentityTheft.gov (official) → 💰

I sent money

Call your bank using the number on your card or statement—not from a text or email. We list official bank fraud numbers on the next page.

Official bank & agency numbers → 📞

I feel overwhelmed or need to report fraud

ReportFraud.ftc.gov is the Federal Trade Commission's official fraud reporting site. For phone support, call the FTC below.

ReportFraud.ftc.gov →

Phone: 1-877-FTC-HELP (1-877-382-4357) — Federal Trade Commission. For crisis support, call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline).
